Mission & Values

Mission

Corporate Finance Institute® (CFI) is a leading global provider of online financial modeling and valuation courses. In 2018, CFI’s programs and certifications were delivered to 103,033 individuals at top universities, investment banks, accounting firms, and operating companies around the world. CFI courses give you practical skills, templates, and tools to advance your career. We pick up where business school leaves off to teach you on-the-job aspects of corporate finance, investment banking, corporate development, FP&A, treasury, and accounting.



Vision

Everyone with access to the internet and the motivation to learn can advance their career with CFI’s online courses and have a rewarding career. We put our students first and act as our compass when developing new training solutions, providing customer support, and upgrading our educational materials. We strive to empower individuals worldwide through world-class education that will help them lead successful careers and fulfilling lives!



Values

Our values are the core beliefs of our global organization. They define our culture and determine the way we engage with our students, create products and services, and grow our community. Our values guide us in our everyday decisions and help to set a high standard of work ethics. Our team consistently lives by these values and strives to build a better future through professional education that is accessible to students worldwide. By sharing high-quality education with students across the world, we work towards creating a life-changing impact in our local and international communities. Through Practical, Simple, Professional, Democratic, and Philanthropic practices, we have created a successful company culture that celebrates innovation, encourages individual growth, and inspires student success.



Culture

Adoption of these fundamental values is essential to growing a community of CFI students and employees who feel proud to be a part of world’s leading financial analyst certification institution.
